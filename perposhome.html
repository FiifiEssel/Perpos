Sustainable Development Goals

The Sustainable Development Goals (SDGs), adopted by all United Nations Member States in 2015, provide a shared blueprint for peace and prosperity for people and the planet, now and into the future.

These 17 interconnected goals aim to address the world's most pressing challenges, including poverty, inequality, climate change, environmental degradation, peace, and justice.
